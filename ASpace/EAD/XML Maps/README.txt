Developer -> Source - > XML Maps -> Add
Drag the items from the right (container 1, container 2, etc.) to spreadsheet columns A, B, C, etc.
Paste in relevant information, click “Export”.
Change c02 level to <c02 level=“file”> in outputted xml.
Change c03 level to <c03 level=“item”> in outputted xml (for item level).
Change <container1> to <container type=“Box”> in outputted xml.
Change <container2> to <container type=“Folder”> in outputted xml.
Change </container1> and </container2> to </container> in outputted xml.
Change <unitdate> to <unitdate type=”inclusive”> in outputted xml.
Paste resulting container list into EAD template.
